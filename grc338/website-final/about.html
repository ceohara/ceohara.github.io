Welcome to Butter & Bloom Bakery, where every bite blossoms with flavor!. Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, we are a boutique bakery specializing in artisanal pastries, cakes, and baked goods that bring joy to every occasion.

At Butter & Bloom, we believe that baking is both an art and a science, blending tradition with creativity to deliver flavors that feel like home. From our buttery croissants to our elegantly designed custom cakes, each creation is made from scratch using high-quality, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Our passionate team of bakers and pastry chefs is dedicated to perfecting classic recipes while also innovating new, delightful treats. Whether you’re celebrating a special milestone, indulging in a daily sweet treat, or looking for the perfect gift, Butter & Bloom Bakery has something to make your moment memorable.

Visit us at our cozy bakery or order online for a taste of handcrafted goodness. We can’t wait to share our love for baking with you!
